A minerals management system (MMS) is a comprehensive set of policies, procedures, and tools designed to manage the exploration, production, and distribution of minerals in an efficient and effective manner.


The system includes processes for identifying, acquiring, developing, and utilizing mineral resources, as well as monitoring and controlling their extraction and use.

It also encompasses the management of environmental, health, and safety risks associated with mineral extraction and processing activities.
The goal of an MMS is to optimize the value of mineral resources while minimizing negative impacts on people, communities, and the environment.

MMS can be used in a variety of contexts, including mining, oil and gas extraction, and mineral exploration and development
